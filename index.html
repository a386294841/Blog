Yesterday Once More.When I was young I'd listen to the radio Waiting for my favorite songs When they played I'd sing along, It make me smile. Those were such happy times and not so long ago How I wondered where they'd gone. But they're back again just like a long lost friend All the songs I love so well. Every shalala every wo'wo still shines.
